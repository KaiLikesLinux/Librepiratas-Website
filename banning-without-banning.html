Legalize Everything

... but without encouraging the bad stuff

Should we legalize meth, cocaine, weed, alcohol, cigarettes, and heroine for anyone of all ages? Absolutely. How do we legalize meth for kids without giving kids meth? What is one thing kids don't have much of? Money. Just give steep taxes based on how dangerous the product is, and have stable and sane regulations on how to handle such material. Lesser drugs like weed, alcohol, and cigarettes should have minor taxes of 100%. Thee others should be dealt with by the states, including minimum ages to consume such products (no older than 35). If it is a victimless crime, it is no crime at all, this includes drugs. Rape, murder, assault/battery, and theft are all still illegal, those are not victimless.
